Journey to the Center of Hawkthorne

An 8bit 2D platformer sprinkled with a few RPG elements.
Based off an episode of NBC's Community that ran back in 2012

Our tools:
  • Lua as the central language.
  • LÖVE2D as our graphics library.
Meta-Features:
  • Cross platform self-updating function built in Lua
  • Automated building and publishing through Github and Travis-CI.
  • Automated exception reporting through our sponsor Sentry.
